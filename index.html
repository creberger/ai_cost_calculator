📋 Cost Model Details (from document analysis)
  • Human Labor (64% of spend): Y1 build = $60,160; ongoing Y2+ = $35,880/yr with 3% annual increase
  • Cloud Infrastructure (6% of spend): AWS ECS containers, RDS, ALB, monitoring = $4,218/yr baseline with 5% annual increase
  • Data & Storage (12% of spend): Vector database (Pinecone) $8,400/yr + S3 storage scaling with corpus growth
  • LLM API Costs (<1% of spend): ~$267/yr for Claude Haiku + Sonnet with optimizations; ~$3,336/yr heavy transcript scenario
  • Contingency: Year 1 = 15% of budget; Years 2-5 = 13% (covers scope changes, rework, inflation)
  • Costs scale sub-linearly with users for labor; linearly for API/storage. Infrastructure shows economy of scale at 100+ users.
